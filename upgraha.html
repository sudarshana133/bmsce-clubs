Description:

Upagraha, the satellite club of BMSCE had an eventful semester that entailed three major events. The students of Structures and Mechanisms, Altitude Control and Determination, On Board Computing and Power Subsystems presented their research papers at the "International Conference on Small Satellites (ICSS-2022)", Punjab, April 29th-30th. A Conference talk, Introduction to Spacecraft Technology and Gaganyaan Mission, by Shri. C. D Shridhar, a retiredscientist of ISRO and a mentor to Upagraha. The talk included insights regarding the launch vehicles, satellitetechnology, and the prestigious and upcoming missions of ISRO.
