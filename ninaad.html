Popular events

Description:

On 27th May, Utsav-Envisage started with "ANTARMUKHI" which was an exhilarating performance given by the 2nd-year members. They performed classical, folk, and filmy genres of music. zVarious music competitions were organized during Utsav-Envisage, viz, Prathidhwani, Gamaka, Varna, and Vistaar.These events involved the participation of vocalists and instrumentalists from different colleges. VTU Youth Festival - Prathibhotsava: One of the most popular cultural and literary fests of Karnataka -Prathibhotsava was held on 3 consecutive days, on 29th, 30th, and 31st July at BMS College of Engineering.Students from different colleges took part in the fest enthusiastically. A plethora of music events was organized,namely, classical vocal solo, light vocal, group song - Indian, folk orchestra, and classical instrumental solo(percussion tala vaidya and non-percussion Swara vaidya).
