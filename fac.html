Popular events

Description:

Fine Arts Club in collaboration with the NSS unit of BMSCE organized a Paper Bag Making Mega Event. The event aimed at distributing paper bags in the festival to create awareness about the effects of plastic. Around 600 paper bags were made and successfully distributed among the vendors. The Fine Arts Club in collaboration with the NSS unit of BMSCE had a fun and creative task to paint the boundarywalls of BMS hostel on 15th April. The enthusiastic volunteers took great interest in painting the walls withslogans, monuments and patterns. The aim was to prevent people from spitting or sticking bills on the walls. Four successful fun-filled activities were conducted during Utsav-Envisage, along with designing models for campusdecorations.
