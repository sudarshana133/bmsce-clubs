Popular events

Description:

Pentagram as a club is not just limited to maths but fun- filled events and activities giving students from different departments a platform to socialize and work together. The club conducted six succesful events in the previous semester. The first three events of the semester were held over 3 days during Utsav-Envisage '22, namely, Infinite Amaze,Battle of Brains, and Under the Hammer. All the 3 events were not only house-full but successful as well. Afterthis, the club conducted its 2nd Recruitment Drive for the freshers, where about 70 students where fetched basedon their skills and aptitude.
