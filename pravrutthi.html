Popular events

Description:

The team started off with a MIME performance in VETOMAC, which proved to be one of the most successful events. A street play was performed on the occasion of International Women's Day in collaboration with NSS at KR PARK. The crowd was overwhelmed with the gospel portrayed in the act. The team participated in CMRIT Fest and competed in three different events, namely Mime, madads, and street play. Three events were hosted during Utsav-Envisage. RAGHU GOWDA (RAGHU VINE STORE) was invited as a judge for the madads event. The other two events were Last Actor Standing (LAS) and Street Play. During the VTU fest, Pravrutthi participated in the one act play and skit events. A theater play was performed by the team at SSMRV. The team grabbed the top spot at the Pravega fest conducted by IISC, a national level Theatre play competition. The year was ended with a Street play on cancer awareness in collaboration with the Rotaract club.
